Pets

Our perfect companions never have fewer than four feet

-Sidonie Gabrielle Colette

Unlike commercial carriers, pets in our PC12 aircraft travel right next to their owner regardless of their weight inside the passenger cabin. You can therefore interact and see each other during the whole course of a trip. This eliminates stress both to the animal and the owner or handler as he knows exactly in what mental state his best friend is in during all the flight.

  • No Weight Limitation to what pets can enter the cabin area and travel with their owner/handler
  • No Separation of pet from their owner during the flight
  • Pet is positioned right next to the owner

What is important to know?

  • Pets on board need to have a Pet Passport
  • Pets on board need to have vaccination stamps based on the regulation of the country of entry
  • Guide Dogs are excluded from many restrictions depending on airport of departure or arrival.
  • In most of the EU pets travelling on an aircraft will needed to possess a readable microchip for identification reasons
  • Pets on board need to be in an EASA approved cage (For sale at all major Pet Shops). Ask your local Pet Shop for a cage fit for European flight. Make sure you go one size up to give your best friend enough space to feel comfortable during flight.

Unfortunately, there is no standard Pan-European legislation regarding pets travelling on an aircraft so when making a booking please ask client services to provide you with a checklist of what needs to be done before the flight to ensure a smooth experience both for you and your best friend depending on where you are travelling to.
