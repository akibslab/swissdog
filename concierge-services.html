Concierge Services

What we offer our clients on demand:

Land Transportation/EIP Service

Door to Door Service

From the moment you make a booking, Swissdog Client Services can arrange your pickup with a Mercedes Limousine or a Mercedes Van which will drive you directly to the General Aviation Terminal. From that point your duty has finished. Your luggage will be taken directly to the aircraft, you will be escorted through VIP immigration services and taken directly to the aircraft ready to take off. Upon landing the same will apply as you will be escorted directly through immigration control and your transportation will be waiting for you with your luggage loaded ready to take you to your destination.

Helicopter Connection

(Greek Islands, Monaco and Beyond)

Swissdog clients which wish to be transported via Helicopter to other locations can request this to our client services. Upon arrival to the airport of choice, you can seamlessly connect via Helicopter which will take you to your destination.

In locations this service is available we connect our clients via Airbus Helicopters (Eurocopter). This service is especially useful for clients wishing to connect to a yacht, remote locations in the Greek Islands or holiday destinations like St. Tropez where traffic jams are very common.
